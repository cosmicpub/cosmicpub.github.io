How the spin wheel works

Each segment on the wheel represents one of your options. When you spin, the wheel rotates randomly and lands on a result.

When to use a decision wheel

  • Group decisions
  • Classroom activities
  • Games and challenges
  • Choosing between multiple options

Why random decision tools help

Sometimes the hardest part of a decision is getting started. A random wheel removes hesitation and helps you take action quickly.
