What Should I Do Today? (Idea Generator)

Get ideas for what to do today instantly. Use this free generator to find fun, productive, or relaxing activities when you don’t know what to do.

How this works

This tool gives you a random activity idea from a large pool of options. It also avoids repeating your most recent picks, so each suggestion feels fresh and useful.

When to use this tool

  • When you're bored and don’t know what to do
  • When you need a quick productive task
  • When you want to try something new
  • When you feel stuck or unmotivated

Tips to get the most out of it

Don’t overthink the result. The point is to take action quickly. Even if the idea isn’t perfect, it can help you build momentum and get out of a rut.
