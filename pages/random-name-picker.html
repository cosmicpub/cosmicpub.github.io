How the random name picker works

This tool takes a list of names and selects one at random. Just paste or type one name per line, then click the button to choose a winner instantly.

You can also turn on the remove option to prevent the same name from being picked again on the next round.

Common ways to use a name picker

  • Choosing students in class
  • Picking raffle or giveaway winners
  • Selecting teams or volunteers
  • Running games and group activities
  • Making fair random choices from a list

Why people use random pickers

A random name picker makes selection feel fast, fair, and simple. Instead of deciding manually, you can let the tool choose for you in seconds.

That makes it useful for teachers, organizers, hosts, and anyone who needs a quick random winner or participant from a list of names.
